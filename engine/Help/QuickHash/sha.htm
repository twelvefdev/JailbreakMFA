SHA algorithms:

SHA-1:
Hashes with SHA-1 algorithm. (40 characters, 160bit)
Pretty fast algorithm, very secure.

SHA-2 256 bit:
Hashes with SHA-2 256bit algorithm. (64 characters, 256bit)
The highest you will ever need to use, if at all, overkill for most situations.

SHA-2 384 bit:
Hashes with SHA-2 384bit algorithm. (92 characters, 384bit)

SHA-2 512 bit:
Hashes with SHA-2 512bit algorithm. (128 characters, 512bit)
Should never be needed, very very secure.
