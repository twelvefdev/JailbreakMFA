Gravity check - up/down/left/right

This condition will check if a brick at a given x,y coordinate has a potential to fall.
It is not as simple as to check if the brick below the given brick is empty. No this condition checks if ANY brick below the current brick is empty. It saves you for alot of looping and is usefull to for example to prevent the player of your game to click a block that is currently falling in any direction.

Examples:
Brick at 5, 7 can fall up

Brick at 3, 4 can fall down

Brick at 9, 6 can fall left

Brick at 2, 8 can fall right
