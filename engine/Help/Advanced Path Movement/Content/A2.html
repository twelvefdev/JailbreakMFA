Remove path node

Will delete the path node. All indexes to the other points will be changed.

So say we have 4 nodes:

1                          3

           2                            4

And we decide to delete node 2. All connections to and from node 2 will be destroyed

1                          3

           X                            4

And the index's will shift down

 

1                          2

                                        3

You probably wont have to worry about this, but if your referring to nodes by there ID number its useful information to remember.
